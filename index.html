About ME

I'm a final-year CSE undergraduate at SRM University KTR, deeply interested in web development and AI-driven solutions. I’ve worked extensively with technologies like the MERN stack, Java, Python, SQL, and React. My recent projects include an e-commerce website built with the MERN stack, a Todo management app, and advanced data analysis for Walmart. Beyond projects, I’ve been actively participating in various hackathons, including one focused on healthcare, where I won an award. I'm also part of my college's Full Stack Technical Club and work with the Electronics Club, having developed a website for them.
